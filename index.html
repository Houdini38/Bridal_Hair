FAQ

Do you travel?

Yes! We're a fully on-location team serving Denver, Broomfield, and venues across Colorado. Travel beyond our home area can be arranged — just ask.

How many people can you style?

From the bride alone to a full bridal party of 13+. Larger parties may add a second stylist to keep your timeline on track.

Where should I book?

Start by requesting availability above. Once your date is confirmed, we'll send a contract to lock everything in.

Do you offer trials?

Absolutely. A preview trial lets us perfect your look and plan the timeline before the big day.

What areas do you serve?

Denver, Broomfield, and surrounding Colorado communities, plus destination requests on a case-by-case basis.

Ready to reserve your date?

We can't wait to be part of your day.
